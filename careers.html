Characteristics:

  • Hands-on interest in developing a real world product from concept to production stages

Battery Systems Engineer

  • This position will report to the Head of Powertrain

Qualifications/Experience:

  • Experience working with lithium-ion battery cells in large-format, high voltage applications
  • Experience with functional safety standards such as ISO 26262
  • History of working with DFMEAs, DVP&Rs and other system analysis and development tools
  • Experience with requirements analysis and development using a requirements management framework such as DOORS, Polarion, etc
  • Capable of creating models, simulations, and analysis tools to solve engineering problems using MATLAB, Mathcad or similar engineering framework

Characteristics:

  • Works well on projects on their own as well as with a team, able to clearly communicate ideas

  • CAD Capability: Minimum 10+ years Catia V5 experience (creating CIW or BIW designs or concepts). Preferably from concept phase through Launch

NC Programmer

VoltaEV is seeking a Sr. NC Programmer to join our CNC Technology team. This is a manufacturing engineering position with a focus on machining complex automotive concepts and prototypes. Work is performed with general direction. The position exercises considerable latitude in determining objectives and approaches on assignments. The candidate will guide design and development of part holding strategies, tooling selection, multi-axis mill and lathe NC Programs, part program simulation (Vericut) and complete setup documentation. Leadership, attention to detail, Team player, and get it done attitude are the character traits found in the professional that will help us fulfil our mission

Responsibilities:

  • Develop NC programs utilizing Siemens NX or Autodesk PowerMILL
  • Practice Run Right First Time programming approach
  • Familiar with lights out manufacturing
  • Develop tooling solutions, as needed, both cutting tools and fixturing
  • Support the CNC Machinists as needed to ensure successful part run
  • Determine and formalize technological parameters through CNC programs and participate in the CNC program archiving process
  • Assure full technological documentation with CNC path, featuring and tooling information
  • Contribute new ideas and positive support to the current process by implementing productivity actions in order to improve the process and respect objectives
  • Optimize CNC programs for cycle time reductions
  • Participate in capital equipment purchases by recommending, specifying, installation coordination, and buy off
  • Actively be involved in solving manufacturing and quality issues and implementing correct actions
  • Assist with operator training initiatives and participate in development of operator training programs

Requirements:
  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited college or university (or a High School Diploma / GED and at least 10 years of CNC programming experience). Siemens NX, or Autodesk PowerMILL CAM programming experience. Must have experience with large format 5 Axis Gantry machines, and have worked with large parts. Experience programming and general operation with Fanuc, Heidenhain TNC530, and Okuma OSP CNC controls. Must be able to quickly adapt to changes and update programs accordingly. Ideal candidate will have extensive background in 3D toolpaths and have worked in an environment where at least 90% of the toolpaths being generated were 3 or 5 axis in nature. Must be able to read and understand NC Code to identify troubleshoot problems at the machine. CAD modeling and drafting experience is required. Previous machine shop experience is desirable a huge plus. Must be self-guided, a thinker, and work well in a high paced Team environment with aggressive project deadlines. Will make presentations of concepts and designs to Leaders and other Teams using PowerPoint, Excel, and other common Microsoft or presentation software tools.

Work Environment/Physical Requirements:
  • Employees in these positions must possess mobility to work in a standard office setting and to use standard office equipment, including a computer; stamina to sit or stand and maintain attention to detail despite interruptions; may occasionally lift/carry/push/pull up to 50 pounds; may require occasional walking, climbing, stooping, crouching, and/or bending; and vision to read printed materials and a computer screen, and hearing and speech to communicate in person and over the telephone. May require the ability to travel by air or auto. May require the use of personal protective equipment such as safety glasses, safety shoes, and shop coat. These positions may be expected to work varying shifts and hours to ensure successful operation of activities in the organization.
